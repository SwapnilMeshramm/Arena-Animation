Frequently Asked Questions

What is 3D Animation?

3D Animation is the process of creating three-dimensional moving images in a digital environment. It involves creating objects, characters, and environments in a 3D space and animating them to create realistic or stylized motion.

What software will I learn in this course?

You will learn industry-standard software including Maya, Blender, Unity, Unreal Engine, Zbrush, Substance Painter, and Quixel for creating professional 3D animations and games.

What career opportunities are available after completing this course?

After completing this course, you can pursue careers as a 3D Animator, Game Artist, Character Modeler, Environment Artist, Technical Artist, or work in game development studios, animation studios, or as a freelance artist.

Do I need any prior experience to join this course?

No prior experience is required. The course is designed for beginners and will teach you everything from the basics to advanced techniques in 3D animation and game development.

Will I get placement assistance after completing the course?

Yes, Arena Animation provides comprehensive placement assistance to help you find opportunities in top animation and game development studios. Our placement cell works closely with industry partners to ensure the best career opportunities for our students.
